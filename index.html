How Your Score is Calculated

1. Average Across Valid Steps

We calculate the average percentage for each category across all completed workflow steps (those totaling exactly 100%).

2. Creative Firewall Score = Enhanceable + Irreplaceable

Your firewall score combines both categories that keep humans in control:

  • Irreplaceable: Your ultimate creative moat - work that originates only from you
  • Enhanceable: AI collaboration where you remain the creative director
3. Why This Matters

Automatable work is vulnerable to replacement. Enhanceable work keeps you in the driver's seat while leveraging AI. Irreplaceable work is your strongest defense - the creative signature that can't be replicated.
